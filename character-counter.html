Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Instagram bio character limit?

Instagram bios have a 150-character limit. This includes letters, numbers, symbols, spaces, and emoji. Each emoji counts as 1-2 characters. Use this counter to check your bio length before pasting.

What is the Twitter/X character limit?

Twitter/X has a 280-character limit for standard posts. URLs count as 23 characters. Emoji count as 2 characters each. Twitter Blue subscribers can post up to 25,000 characters.

How many characters can a TikTok bio have?

TikTok bios have an 80-character limit. Captions allow up to 2,200 characters. Comments are limited to 150 characters.
