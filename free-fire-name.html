Enter your name and get 100+ stylish gaming names. Works in Free Fire, PUBG, MLBB & all mobile games.

How to Use Stylish Names in Free Fire

1. Type Your Name

Enter your desired gaming name in the box above.

2. Copy a Style

Click any generated name to copy it to your clipboard.

3. Paste in Game

Open Free Fire → Profile → Edit Name → Paste → Confirm.

You need a Name Change Card to change your Free Fire name. Get one from the in-game store or through events and rewards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my name style in Free Fire?

Open Free Fire → Profile → Edit Name → Paste the stylish name → Confirm. You need a Name Change Card from the store or events.

What symbols work in Free Fire names?

Free Fire supports decorated brackets ꧁꧂, special chars ༒ ☬, gothic letters, and many Unicode symbols. This generator only uses verified symbols.

Do these names work in PUBG and MLBB?

Yes. Most names work in PUBG Mobile, Mobile Legends, COD Mobile, and other games.
