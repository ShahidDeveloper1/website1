What is Vaporwave Text?

Vaporwave text (also called fullwidth text or aesthetic text) replaces standard ASCII characters with their fullwidth Unicode equivalents. Originally designed for East Asian typography where Latin characters need to match the width of CJK characters, the vaporwave/aesthetic internet culture adopted it for its distinctive s p a c e d o u t look.

The style is associated with the vaporwave aesthetic movement — featuring neon colors, '80s/'90s nostalgia, Japanese text, and retro-futuristic visuals. It's widely used in Discord servers, Tumblr, Reddit, and social media bios.

FAQ

What makes vaporwave text different?
Vaporwave text uses fullwidth Unicode characters (U+FF01–U+FF5E) which are wider than normal characters, giving that distinctive spaced-out aesthetic look.
Does vaporwave text work on Discord?
Yes! Fullwidth characters work perfectly on Discord for usernames, messages, and server channel names.
