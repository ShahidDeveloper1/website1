About Dot Symbols

Dot symbols are versatile punctuation and decorative characters used across text, math, and design. From simple bullet points (•) to ellipses (…) and circular shapes (●○), they add style and structure to any text.

Use dot symbols in social media bios, text art borders, separators, or to create dreamy aesthetic vibes in your posts and usernames.
