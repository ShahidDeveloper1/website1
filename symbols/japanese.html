About Japanese Symbols

These symbols comprise the Japanese syllabaries: Hiragana (used for native Japanese words and grammar) and Katakana (used primarily for loanwords, emphasis, and onomatopoeia). You can use these characters to add a unique aesthetic, commonly known as "kana text," to your social media bios, nicknames, and creative designs.
