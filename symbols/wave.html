About Wave Symbols

Wave symbols and text waves are some of the most aesthetic decorative patterns in text art. By combining dots, asterisks, tildes, and other punctuation marks, you can create flowing, water-like effects.

These wavy text dividers are perfect for organizing your Tumblr, Instagram, or TikTok bio, creating beautiful borders for text posts, or just adding a "soft" and pleasing aesthetic to your messages.
