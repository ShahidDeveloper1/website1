About Corner Symbols

Corner symbols are text characters originally designed for creating boxes and tables in text-based environments. They come in light, heavy, double, and rounded variants. You can copy and paste these to create beautiful text borders, UI elements, and highly customized layouts for your social media posts or console interfaces.
