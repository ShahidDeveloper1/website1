About Cryptocurrency Symbols

Cryptocurrency symbols are used to represent digital assets and blockchain networks. The most famous is the Bitcoin symbol (₿), which became an official Unicode character in 2017.

Other coins often use existing Unicode characters that resemble their logos, such as the Greek uppercase Xi (Ξ) for Ethereum, the Mongolian Tugrik (₮) for Tether/USDT, the uppercase Eth (Ð) for Dogecoin, or the Polish L with stroke (Ł) for Litecoin.
