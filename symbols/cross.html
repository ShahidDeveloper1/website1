About Cross Symbols

This master list of cross symbols includes religious icons, bold mathematical operators, and handy interface "X" markers! The first section features prominent Christian and occult symbols such as the Latin cross (✝), the 3D-styled shadow cross (✞), the Eastern cross (☦), and the Ankh (☥). The second section is perfect for UI design, featuring heavy X crosses (✖), red error signs (❌), checkboxes (☒), and curly styles. Just click to copy your favorite to the clipboard!
