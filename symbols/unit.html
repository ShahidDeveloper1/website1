About Unit Measurement Symbols

Unit symbols are highly useful characters that condense measurements into a single unicode block character! Instead of typing multiple letters, you can just paste perfectly formatted, single-character versions of units like Celsius (℃), kilograms (㎏), or millimeters (㎜). Essential for science, math, coding, or neat reporting!
