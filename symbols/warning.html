About Warning Symbols

Warning symbols are universally recognized icons used to indicate potential hazards, restrictions, or prohibited actions. This collection includes critical alert emojis like radioactive or biohazard symbols, alongside everyday restriction signs like "No Phones", "No Smoking", and "No Entry". Both standard text symbols and fully colored emojis are included here!
