About Bracket Symbols

Brackets and punctuation marks are some of the most useful text formatting tools! This comprehensive library provides much more than your standard `{ }` and `< >` brackets. Here, you can copy the thick Japanese Lenticular brackets (【 】), wide double angle brackets (《 》), vertically aligned parenthesis (︵ ︶), and various CJK punctuation marks (々 〆). Perfect for aesthetic usernames, formatting code, or bolding text headers!
