About Triangle Symbols

Triangle symbols and emojis are incredibly versatile elements used for directional indicators, mathematical notation, and beautiful vertical separators. The special enclosing triangle (⃤ ) is especially popular on social media to create aesthetic geometric frame effects around emojis (like 🦋⃤ ).
