About Divider Symbols

Divider symbols and text borders are essential for structuring your social media posts, bios (specifically on TikTok, Instagram, and Tumblr), and formatting Discord servers.

Using these combinations of lines `─`, dots `·`, sparkles `✨`, stars `⋆`, and celestial symbols `☁` creates a neat, aesthetic separation between different sections of your text while maintaining a pleasing visual style.
