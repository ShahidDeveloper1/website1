About Loading Symbols

Loading symbols and progress bar text art use Unicode block characters (█▓▒░) to create visual loading bars. These are popular in gaming profiles, Discord statuses, social media bios, and creative text art.

Use them to show progress, add drama to your posts, or create a retro "computer loading" aesthetic in your username or bio!
