About Old English Font

Old English font, technically known as Fraktur or Blackletter, is a style of script used for manuscript books and documents throughout Europe during the Middle Ages. The Unicode character sets include these letters primarily for mathematical notation, but they've become widely popular online for creating "Old English" style text on Twitter, Instagram, and Discord.
