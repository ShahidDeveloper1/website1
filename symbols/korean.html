About Korean Symbols

Korean Hangul symbols represent one of the most scientifically designed writing systems in the world. Created in the 15th century by King Sejong, these characters combine consonants and vowels in elegant syllable blocks that are both functional and visually appealing for decorative text.

On Fancy Text, we provide a carefully curated collection of korean text symbols that you can copy and paste anywhere. These Unicode characters are part of the universal text standard, which means they work on every device and platform without installing any fonts or apps. Whether you are on an iPhone, Android, Windows PC, or Mac, these korean text symbols will display correctly every time.
