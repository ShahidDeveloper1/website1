About Music Symbols

Music makes the world go round! 🎼 If you want to decorate your social media bio, write about your favorite songs, or share sheet music online, this page has standard Unicode representations for almost all basic musical notations. You can easily click to copy quarter and eighth notes (♩♪), chords (♫), and sharps/flats (♯♭). This page also collects the most popular music emojis representing instruments like guitars (🎸), keyboards (🎹), and various audio settings (🔊)!
