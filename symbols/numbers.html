About Number Symbols

Numbers don't have to be boring! With this master list of number symbols, you can grab elegant Roman numerals (Ⅰ, Ⅱ, Ⅲ), aesthetically pleasing circled digits (①, ❷), ready-to-use lists with dots and parentheses (⑴, ⒈), and even fractional characters (½, ¾) straight from the unicode library. Superscript and subscript options (¹ ₂, etc.) are especially useful for writing math equations, footnotes, or adding flair to your username or bio!
