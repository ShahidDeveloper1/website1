About Gender Symbols

This page features a robust collection of Unicode gender markers! It includes the classic Venus (♀) representing female and Mars (♂) representing male, which derive from ancient astrology. The list has since expanded to include the male and female intersecting sign (⚥), the transgender marker (⚧), and various combinations to represent specific sexualities, orientations, and relationships like the interlocking male and female sign (⚤) or double female (⚢).
