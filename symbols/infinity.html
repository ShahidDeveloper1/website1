About Infinity Symbols

Originally from mathematics, the infinity symbol (∞) is now widely used in text to represent endlessness, romance, and aesthetic designs! You can easily copy the classic text infinity, the heavier Permanent Paper emoji (♾), or use specialized math variants like the Incomplete Infinity (⧜) or Tie Over Infinity (⧝). These are extremely popular for usernames, couples bios, and stylish quotes.
