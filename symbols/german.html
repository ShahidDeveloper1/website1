About German Symbols

The German alphabet uses the standard 26 letters of the Latin alphabet, plus three umlauted vowels (Ä, Ö, Ü) and the Eszett (ß), which represents a sharp "s" sound. This page also includes standard typography marks commonly used in the German language, such as specialized quotation marks (Guillemets).
