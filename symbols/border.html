About Border Symbols

Border symbols (often called box-drawing characters) are special Unicode characters designed to draw geometric lines and boxes in text applications. They are extremely popular for making aesthetic text boxes, quote containers, and bio layouts.

By combining corners `в•”`, `в•—` and straight lines `в•җ`, `в•‘` with cute symbols like stars and flowers, you can create beautiful frames for your social media profiles, Tumblr posts, and Discord messages.
