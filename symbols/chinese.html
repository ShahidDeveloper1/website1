About Chinese Symbols

Chinese characters (Hanzi) are logograms used in writing Chinese. The enclosed ideographs on this page are specially formatted characters with circles around them, often used in Japanese and Chinese typography to represent days of the week, honorifics, or specific concepts. These characters are perfect for enhancing your social media bios, nicknames, or graphic designs.
