Frequently Asked Questions

How do I copy medal symbols on mobile?
Simply tap on any medal or achievement symbol on our site. It will be copied to your clipboard instantly. You can then open your favorite app (like Instagram or TikTok) and tap inside the text box to paste it. No extra apps or keyboards are needed.
Do medal text symbols work on Instagram and Discord?
Yes. Because all of our medal symbols are standardized Unicode characters, they display perfectly on Instagram, Discord, TikTok, WhatsApp, and all other modern communication channels and web browsers.
What is the difference between trophies and medals?
A trophy 🏆 represents a team or individual victory for an entire tournament or competition. Medals, such as 🥇, 🥈, and 🥉, represent specific individual rankings (first, second, and third place) or honorary achievements 🎖️ given out to participants. Both are excellent for celebrating success!
