Frequently Asked Questions

What symbols work in Instagram usernames?

Instagram usernames only allow letters, numbers, periods, and underscores. However, Instagram display names support Unicode symbols. Use the generated names in your profile name field.

Can I use symbols in Discord usernames?

Yes. Discord display names support Unicode characters including stylized letters, symbols, and emoji.

What are good aesthetic usernames?

Good aesthetic usernames combine a meaningful word with decorative Unicode symbols. Examples: ✦ luna ✦, ꒰ angel ꒱, ☾ mystic ☾. This generator creates personalized names from any word.
