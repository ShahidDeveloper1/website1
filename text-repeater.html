Text Repeater

Repeat a word, phrase, or emoji up to 100,000 times instantly. Perfect for WhatsApp, Discord, or sending long messages.

What is a Text Repeater?

A Text Repeater is an online utility that allows you to multiply a specific string of text, word, or emoji as many times as you want. Instead of manually copying and pasting multiple times, you can simply type your message once, set the repetition count (e.g., 100, 10,000, or even 100,000 times), and generate a massive wall of text in milliseconds.

How to Use This Tool

  • Type your message: It can be a single word, a sentence, or a string of emojis (like "I'm sorry", "Happy Birthday 🎂", or just "a").
  • Set the limit: Enter how many times you want it repeated. Our tool handles up to 100,000 repetitions without crashing your browser.
  • Choose format: You can add a space, comma, or any other separator between repetitions. You can also force each repetition onto a new line, or add numbers (1. 2. 3.) to make a numbered list.
  • Copy and send: Click the copy button and paste it into WhatsApp, Discord, iMessage, or Instagram.
