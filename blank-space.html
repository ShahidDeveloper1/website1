What Is a Blank Space Character?

A blank space character is a special Unicode character that appears completely empty but is still recognized as text by apps and websites. Unlike pressing the spacebar (which creates a standard space), these invisible characters can create truly blank messages, empty usernames, and invisible text separators.

How to Use Blank Spaces

1. Click Copy

Click any "Copy" button above. The blank character is now on your clipboard.

2. Paste

Go to WhatsApp, Instagram, Discord, or any app. Press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac).

3. Send/Save

The text will appear blank. Send the message or save your profile.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send a blank message on WhatsApp?

Copy the blank space character from this page, open WhatsApp, paste it into the message field, and press send. The Hangul Filler (ㅤ) works most reliably.

How do I make a blank Instagram bio?

Copy blank space characters and paste them into your bio. Use Braille Blank (⠀) or Hangul Filler (ㅤ) for best results.

Can I make a blank Discord name?

Yes. Use the Hangul Filler (ㅤ) in your display name settings.

How do I copy blank space?

Click any 'Copy' button on this page. The blank character is copied to your clipboard. Then paste (Ctrl+V) wherever you need it.
