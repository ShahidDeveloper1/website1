🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Information We Collect

TextSymbols is a static website that provides copy-and-paste symbols and font generation tools. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from our users.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. All symbol copying and font generation happens locally in your browser.

Third-Party Services

We may use Google Fonts for typography. Google Fonts may collect limited usage data as described in Google's privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Data Storage

No data is stored on our servers. All operations (copy to clipboard, font generation) are performed client-side in your web browser.

Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Contact page.
