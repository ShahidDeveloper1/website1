🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction and Scope

Welcome to Fancy Text (available at fancysymbols.com). Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we do not collect, how our tools operate purely on the client side, and our commitment to maintaining a secure, private, and transparent environment for all visitors. This policy applies to all pages, font generators, and copy-and-paste utility services offered on our platform.

2. A Fully Client-Side Architecture

Fancy Text is engineered as a static web application. Unlike traditional platforms that process user input on remote servers, all utility tools on our site—including the Preppy Font Generator, Cute Font Generator, Aesthetic Fonts, Lenny Faces, and our vast library of copy-and-paste text symbols—operate entirely within your local web browser. When you enter text, select a style, or copy a symbol, no data is sent to our servers. The conversion of standard text into fancy Unicode styles and the clipboard copying actions are processed using client-side JavaScript, ensuring complete local execution.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

Because our services operate locally, we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require account registration, email sign-ups, or login credentials to access our premium tools. You are free to browse all 228+ pages of symbols, customize your social media bios, and copy content without ever disclosing your name, email address, IP address, or location. We believe in absolute utility without tracking.

4. Cookies and Local Browser Storage

Fancy Text does not deploy tracking cookies, advertising beacons, or pixel tags to monitor your web activity. We do not use persistent cookies to profile our users or sell behavioral data to third-party marketing firms. Any preference tracking (such as saving your recently copied symbols or active theme preferences) is handled using standard browser local storage (LocalStorage). This data remains strictly stored on your own device and can be cleared at any time by wiping your browser history or cache.

5. Third-Party Integrations and CDN Services

To ensure rapid loading times and premium typography, we integrate standard, reliable third-party resources. Specifically, we load Google Fonts for professional font rendering and use content delivery networks (CDNs) to serve stylesheet assets. These third-party services may automatically capture basic technical information, such as your user-agent string or IP address, solely for the purpose of serving requested assets and maintaining network security. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle font requests.

6. Security and Data Protection

Even though we do not store personal data on our servers, we prioritize user security. Our entire site is served over HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure) utilizing modern SSL/TLS encryption. This ensures that the connection between your browser and our static servers is fully encrypted and protected against unauthorized interception or tampering. Since no personal data is transferred, there is no risk of data breaches or leaks on our end.

7. Children's Online Privacy Protection

Our website and static text utilities are designed for users of all ages, including children, teenagers, and professional creators. We do not knowingly solicit, collect, or process information from children under the age of 13. Because our platform is completely free of user registration and personal tracking, we maintain full compliance with children's online privacy protection regulations worldwide.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update or revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect technological improvements, security upgrades, or changes in regulatory standards. Any updates will be posted directly on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. We recommend checking this page occasionally to stay informed about our ongoing commitment to privacy.

9. Contact and Feedback

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns regarding our privacy practices, or if you need assistance with any of our digital tools, please feel free to reach out. We value your feedback and are always looking for ways to improve your user experience.
