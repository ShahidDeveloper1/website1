What is Gothic Text?

Gothic text (also called Fraktur, blackletter, or Old English) uses Unicode characters from the Mathematical Fraktur block. These characters mimic the medieval calligraphy style used in manuscripts and early printed books across Europe. There are two variations: regular Fraktur (𝔄𝔅ℭ) and bold Fraktur (𝕬𝕭𝕮).

Gothic fonts are extremely popular for gaming usernames, Discord servers, Instagram bios, tattoo text previews, and dark aesthetic content.
