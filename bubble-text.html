What Are Bubble Letters?

Bubble letters (also called circled letters) are Unicode characters where each letter appears inside a circle. They come from the Enclosed Alphanumerics block (U+2460–U+24FF) and the Enclosed Alphanumeric Supplement block. There are several variations including light circles ⓐ, dark/filled circles 🅐, and squared letters 🄰.

These characters work everywhere — Instagram, Discord, TikTok, Twitter, and any app that supports Unicode. They're perfect for making your text stand out in bios, usernames, and messages.

FAQ

Do bubble letters work on Instagram?
Yes! Bubble letters are Unicode characters that work perfectly on Instagram bios, captions, and comments.
Why don't some letters have bubble versions?
Numbers and lowercase letters have official Unicode bubble/circle versions. Some special characters may not have circled equivalents and will appear unchanged.
