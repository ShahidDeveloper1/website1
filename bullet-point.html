Frequently Asked Questions

How do I type a bullet point?

On Windows: Hold Alt and type 0149 on the numpad. On Mac: Press Option+8. On phone: Copy from this page and paste.

What is the keyboard shortcut for bullet point?

Windows: Alt+0149 or Alt+7. Mac: Option+8. Google Docs: Ctrl+Shift+8. Word: Ctrl+Shift+L.
