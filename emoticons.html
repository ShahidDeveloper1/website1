What Are Kaomoji & Text Emoticons?

Kaomoji (顔文字) are Japanese-style text emoticons made entirely from Unicode characters. Unlike Western emoticons like :) and :( that are read sideways, kaomoji are read face-on — like (◕‿◕) for happy or (╥_╥) for crying. They originated in Japanese internet culture in the 1980s and are now used worldwide.

All emoticons on this page are plain text — they copy and paste into any app, message, bio, or document without needing special fonts or plugins.
